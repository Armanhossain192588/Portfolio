About Me

Hello! I'm Arman Hossain, a passionate and results-driven Software Developer, Frontend Engineer, and Project Coordinator with a proven track record of delivering high-quality software solutions and managing complex projects. With a strong foundation in computer science and hands-on experience in web development, I specialize in creating seamless, user-friendly, and visually appealing applications.

Currently, I am working as a Project Coordinator at RA Consulting, where I manage and oversee the full lifecycle of client applications for EB2, NIW, and EB1 processes. I ensure efficient project execution, maintain clear communication with clients, and deliver accurate and timely results. My role involves guiding applicants through the entire process, from initial consultation to final submission, ensuring compliance with guidelines and meeting deadlines.

I have also gained valuable experience as a Software Developer at Acnova Solutions Ltd., where I provided technical support, maintained software systems, and developed tailored solutions to enhance business processes. Beyond my professional work, I am the CEO and Co-Founder of Denebion, a software tech company focused on leveraging AI, Machine Learning, and custom software development to solve real-world problems. Starting with small projects on Fiverr, I am now working towards scaling the company to develop larger, more impactful tech solutions.

I hold a Bachelor's Degree in Computer Science from Northern University, Bangladesh, with a current CGPA of 3.85. My academic excellence is complemented by certifications in Frontend Development, Machine Learning, and Microsoft Office. I am also an active member of the Northern University Bangladesh Computer Club (NUBCC) and have held leadership roles in various organizations.
